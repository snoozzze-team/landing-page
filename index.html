What is Snoozzze?

Snoozzze is an app that will help you track how much you sleep each night. Additionally the app will ask you how you feel when you wake, how you felt during the day, and how tired you are before you go to bed using emojis. The emojis are each given a numerical value that is added up to calcualte your daily score. If you make a mistake or forget to log a day the app will let you go back and edit any previously entered data.

How does it benefit me?

Snoozzze will take your logged sleep each day you wake up and compare it to your emoji score for that day and create a line graph presenting you the data. After you use snoozzze for a whole month the app will then be able to suggest how much sleep is best for you.
